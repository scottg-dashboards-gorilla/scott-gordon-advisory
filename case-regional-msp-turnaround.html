CASE 01 / FRACTIONAL COO Regional MSP · ~$24M revenue

From scaling chaos to operating control.

Revenue had nearly doubled in three years, but profit was flat and execution was unraveling. Everything routed through the founder. I stepped in as fractional COO to build the operating system the business had outgrown.

What I did

01

Rebuilt the P&L

Re-cut the chart of accounts and cost allocations so margin could be read by service line, client, and team — accurately.

02

Installed the operating cadence

A weekly scorecard, clear owners, and a meeting rhythm that surfaced problems early instead of at quarter close.

03

Got the founder out of the loop

Pushed decisions to accountable owners with the guardrails to make them — freeing the founder to lead, not firefight.

04

Made the data live

Replaced the six-week reporting lag with dashboards leadership could act on the same day — the foundation for AI later.
