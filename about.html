ABOUT

Seeing what leadership can't — then fixing it.

An operator, not just an advisor. We step inside growing businesses, find what's holding them back, and stay to fix it.

We serve as a fractional COO/President for several growth-stage companies and coach the CEOs who lead them. Our core value is seeing what leadership can't: surfacing blind spots in how the business is actually running, pinpointing the gaps and core challenges, and then filling and fixing them.

We roll up our sleeves to dig deep into how a business truly operates — closing gaps in visibility, data integrity, and execution while strengthening leadership and culture. We drive accountability across teams, evolve how a company works, and scale leadership so the business can grow without losing control of how it runs.

With experience building and selling a large IT services firm, we launched C-Level Advising in 2021 to help other CEOs navigate the challenges of growth. We specialize in streamlining operations, improving profitability, building exit-ready businesses, and applying AI as a practical lever for performance — not a buzzword.

A central part of our work today is helping companies launch AI the right way — enabling users, protecting the business, ensuring data integrity, and overseeing safe usage. We've also become an expert in AI virtual employees: agents that run 24/7 at a fraction of the cost of traditional headcount.
