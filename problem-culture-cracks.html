PROBLEM 07

Growth is straining how your team works.

The scrappy magic that got you here is fading. New hires don’t absorb it by osmosis, communication is fraying, and the team feels the difference even if no one names it.

SIGNS YOU HAVE IT

Does this sound familiar?

  • ✓The early energy is fading
  • ✓New hires don’t “get it” the way the first team did
  • ✓Silos and friction between groups
  • ✓Values feel like a poster, not a practice
WHAT'S REALLY GOING ON

Culture doesn’t break loudly — it erodes as headcount grows and the informal ways of working stop scaling.

Evolving culture deliberately, rather than letting it drift, is part of scaling leadership. It can be strengthened on purpose.

How we fix it

THE WORK
01

Diagnose the drift

Find where growth has strained how the team actually works.

02

Re-anchor what matters

Make values operational — visible in how decisions get made.

03

Scale the leaders

Equip managers to carry culture as the team grows.

Keep what made you great as you scale.
